Helping students overcome modular learning challenges

Modular and asynchronous learning often leaves students studying independently with limited support. EduAlly bridges that gap with a Socratic AI helper that provides guided explanations and step-by-step scaffolding instead of just answers.

The platform also includes a safe, collaborative forum where learners can ask questions, share ideas, and get support without the pressure of public judgment.
